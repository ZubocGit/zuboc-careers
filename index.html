  • Strong communication and leadership skills

Crafted with Heart, Led by Vision

Zuboc began as a quiet love for calligraphy and blossomed into a vibrant world of handcrafted gifting. Founded by Ayisha Nitha and inspired by her father's entrepreneurial legacy, Zuboc is a father-daughter dream that blends creativity with purpose. Every box, ribbon, and detail is designed to spark joy — because at Zuboc, we believe in the art of making people happy.
